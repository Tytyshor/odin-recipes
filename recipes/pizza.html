Pizza

Pizza is an Italian food that was created in Italy (The Naples area). It is made with different toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2½ cups organic all-purpose flour ((300 grams)
  • 1 teaspoon dry active yeast
  • ½ cup warm water
  • 1½ tablespoon olive oil
  • ⅓ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Dissolve the yeast and sugar in 1/2 c warm water. Let it stand for about 5 minutes.
  2. Now add the 4 3/4 c flour, oil, salt and the 1 1/2 c water into the yeast mixture.
  3. Coat a bowl with 1 tbsp of olive oil. Place the dough in the bowl and turn over to coat all sides.
  4. Cover the bowl and then place in an oven that just has the oven light turned on for 45 minutes.
  5. Cut the dough into four equal pieces and form into balls.
  6. Cover the dough again and place in the fridge for 30 minutes.
  7. Form the pizza
  8. Bake in an oven that's been preheated to 500° for 5 minutes.
  9. Remove the dough from the oven, and to with sauce and toppings.
  10. Place back in the oven and back for 8 minutes, or until the crust is golden. Slice and enjoy!
